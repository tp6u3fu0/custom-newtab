隱私權政策

最後更新：2026 年 6 月

資料收集

Custom New Tab 擴充功能不會收集、儲存或傳送任何個人資料給開發者。

本地儲存

所有設定（外觀、背景、時鐘格式等）與 API 暫存資料，均儲存於您瀏覽器的本機 localStorage，不會上傳至任何伺服器。

位置資訊

天氣模組會在您授權後取得您的裝置位置，僅用於向 Open-Meteo 查詢天氣資料。位置資訊不會傳送給開發者，亦不會被記錄或儲存。

第三方服務

本擴充功能會依據您啟用的模組，連線至以下第三方服務：

服務用途
open-meteo.com天氣資料
api.jolpi.caF1 賽事資料（Ergast）
flagcdn.com國旗圖片
leetcode.comLeetCode 解題進度

以上服務均有各自的隱私權政策，本擴充功能不對其內容負責。

聯絡方式

如有任何疑問，請透過 GitHub Issues 與我聯絡。

Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

Data Collection

Custom New Tab does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data to the developer.

Local Storage

All settings and cached API responses are stored locally in your browser's localStorage and are never uploaded to any server.

Location

The weather module may request your device location (only with your explicit permission) to fetch weather data from Open-Meteo. Your location is never sent to the developer or logged in any way.

Third-Party Services

Depending on which modules you enable, the extension may connect to the following third-party services:

ServicePurpose
open-meteo.comWeather data
api.jolpi.caF1 race data (Ergast)
flagcdn.comCountry flag images
leetcode.comLeetCode problem statistics

Each service has its own privacy policy. The developer is not responsible for their content.
